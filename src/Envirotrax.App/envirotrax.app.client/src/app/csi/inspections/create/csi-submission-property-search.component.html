Before you begin entering the results of your inspection, you must search to see if the property record is on file. You may search for an existing property by either the account number or the property address.

If searching by the address, enter the property street number, street name, and suite/bldg/room number if applicable. Then, click the Search button to bring up all the property records matching your criteria.

  • Only the street number field is required for searching.
  • Do not enter directional prefixes such as North, South, East, West, etc.  Existing property records might have different abbreviations and spellings which may cause the search results to be incomplete.
  • Do not enter street suffixes such as St, Blvd, Cir, Rd, etc.  Existing property records might have different abbreviations and spellings which may cause the search results to be incomplete.
