Please read the following important information on how to register and manage insurance policies

Adding Insurance Policies

When registering an insurance policy, you must upload an electronic copy of Certificate of Insurance (COI) by clicking the Choose File button and selecting the file to upload. Once received, our staff will validate the policy by entering the expiration date.

Verify that all information is correct, then click the Save button to submit policy for review.

Renewing An Insurance Policies

To renew an insurance policy, enter the new policy number and upload an updated Certificate of Insurance (COI). Policies are typically validated within an hour, but may take up to one business day in rare cases.

Reinstating Expired Insurance Policies

If your insurance policy has expired, you must obtain a renewed policy from your insurance provider before reinstating it in the system. Once you have the updated policy, contact us so our staff can verify and update it.

Please click on the Choose File button below and select the Certificate of Insurance (COI) you wish to upload.

Upload a new Certificate of Insurance (COI) only if you want to replace the existing file.
