Please read the following important information on how to register and manage your gauges

Register your gauge

Click the Register New Gauge button below to register a new gauge with your account. A new blank gauge will be added to the Gauge Management section above. Please enter the gauge's Manufacturer, Model, Serial Number, and click the OK button. This will complete the registration, however, your gauge will still need to be validated by Vepo personnel before it can be used.

Gauge validation

For Test for Accuracy Reports (TFA) you must upload an electronic copy of your Test for Accuracy Report to your account by clicking on the Choose File button and selecting the TFA file to upload. The gauge will not be available for use until the test for accuracy expiration date is entered by Vepo personnel into the system. Test for accuracy reports are normally validated within an hour, but could take as long as one business day on rare occasions.

Please click on the Choose File button below and select the Test for Accuracy Report (TFA) you wish to upload.
