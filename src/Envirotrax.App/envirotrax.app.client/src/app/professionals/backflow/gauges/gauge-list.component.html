Please read the following important information on how to register and manage your gauges

Adding gauges

When registering a gauge, enter the manufacturer, model, and serial number along with the last calibration date. This information is used to track and validate gauges associated with your backflow tests.

Verify that all information is correct, then click the Register New Gauge button to add your gauge to the system.

Keeping calibration dates current

Gauges must have a current calibration date to be used in backflow tests. Use the edit button on an existing gauge to update the last calibration date after recalibration. Gauges with outdated calibration dates may not be accepted when submitting backflow tests.
