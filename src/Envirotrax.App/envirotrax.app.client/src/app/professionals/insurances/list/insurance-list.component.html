Please read the following important information on how to register and manage your insurance policies

Adding Insurance Policies

When registering an insurance policy, you must upload an electronic copy of your Certificate of Insurance (COI) by clicking the Choose File button and selecting the file to upload. Once received, our staff will validate the policy by entering the expiration date.

Verify that all information is correct, then click the Register New Insurance Policy button to submit your policy for review.

Renewing Your Insurance Policies

To renew an insurance policy, enter the new policy number and upload an updated Certificate of Insurance (COI). Policies are typically validated within an hour, but may take up to one business day in rare cases.

Reinstating Expired Insurance Policies

If your insurance policy has expired, you must obtain a renewed policy from your insurance provider before reinstating it in the system. Once you have the updated policy, contact us so our staff can verify and update it.
