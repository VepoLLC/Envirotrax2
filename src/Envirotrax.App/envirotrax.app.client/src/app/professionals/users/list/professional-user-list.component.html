User Management

If this is a company account employing more than one inspector, you will need to create a sub-account for each inspector.

This account will then be considered the "Master" (owner/manager) account. Each employed inspector can then login to their sub-account with the User ID (email address) and Password that they create when they acceopt the invitation to register. Below are the advantages of creating sub-accounts for all inspectors employed by the Master account holder:

  • Sub-accounts will automatically be registered with any water supplier in which the Master account is registered.
  • If an inspector with a sub-account employment is terminated, you can remove the sub-account from the Master account at any time. All inspections submitted under the removed sub-account will be moved to the Master account and will no longer be available to the sub-account. The removed sub-account will be considered independent of the Master account and will no longer share the Master account's water supplier registrations, licenses, or insurance.
